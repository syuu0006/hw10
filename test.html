2019 World Happiness Ranking

This visualization will show you the happiness level for countries in 2019 as well as give you an idea of how do society, urban and natural environment affect our happiness

Distribution of countries with different level of happiness

This histogram displays the distribution with different level of happiness. Higher happiness score indicates that the people in this country feel happier.

From this chart, we notice that most countries have Happiness Score between 4.4-6.4

Correlation between Factors and Happiness Score

This chart shows to what extent different factor is affecting on people's happiness.

Factors include GDP per capita, Perceptions of corruption, Generosity, Freedom to make life choices, Healthy life expectancy and Social support.

Each point indicates a single country and different colours represent they belong to different continents.

GDP per capita for Countries

This map chart is mainly about the score of GDP per capita for different countries.

This chart uses colour saturation to indicates different level of GDP per capita.

The darker colour, the higher GDP a country has.

Hover over the map to check the accurate score for different countries.

Happiness Score vs. GDP vs. Population

This bubble chart can help you get a general idea of how GPD affects People's happiness as well as help you discover the happiness level of countries in different continent with different level of population.

Try to use the selection filter to see data for a particular contient.
